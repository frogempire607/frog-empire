Community Mission

Our Non-Profit

We believe every child deserves access to elite coaching regardless of financial circumstances. Our non-profit arm makes that possible.

Our Belief

Every Kid Deserves a Chance

At Frog Empire, we believe that children are powerful. When supported by strong role models and a structured system, they can grow into leaders — on and off the mat.

Our non-profit initiative is focused on removing financial barriers to participation. No athlete should miss out on elite coaching simply because of economic circumstance. Through community partnerships, donations, and grant funding, we work to ensure that every kid who wants to wrestle gets the chance.

We also partner with Empire Wrestling Academy whose legacy and curriculum helped launch Frog Empire's Ithaca expansion.
