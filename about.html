About Frog Empire

Our Story

Redefining the Way We Teach Wrestling

Frog Empire Ithaca Wrestling Academy was born from a lifelong dream. Since the age of seven, wrestling has been more than just a sport — it's been a defining force in Julian's life. It instilled discipline, resilience, and a mindset that anything is possible through hard work.

His journey took a transformative turn at Cornell University, where he pursued not just national wrestling success but also academic development, earning minors in Business and Leadership. Through years of intense training, mentorship, and growth, he developed a deep passion for coaching and helping others unlock their full potential.

Opening a wrestling club has always been his vision: to create a space where kids believe in themselves, build confidence, and learn life lessons through the sport. That vision became reality when Empire Wrestling Academy reached out and offered their support in launching this Ithaca-based expansion.

Mission

Children Are Powerful

At Frog Empire Wrestling Academy, we believe every child holds the power to change the world. Through elite coaching, unwavering support, and a relentless belief in their potential, we help young athletes discover strength — on the mat and in life. Our mission is to build confidence, character, and champions by giving every kid a chance to rise.
